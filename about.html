About Karigar

We are a trusted platform connecting skilled home service providers with customers who need reliable, high-quality services.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Karigar was born from the simple idea that finding reliable home service professionals shouldn't be a hassle. We started as a small team passionate about bridging the gap between skilled karigars (artisans) and homeowners who value quality work.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of customers across the region, making home maintenance easier, faster, and more transparent.

Our Mission

To empower skilled professionals with steady work opportunities while providing customers with trustworthy, affordable, and convenient home services.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and preferred platform for home services, where quality craftsmanship meets modern convenience.
